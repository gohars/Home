Snacks And Sweets

Fruit Chaat

Fruit chaat is a dish consisting of various kinds of fruits cut into small cubes and is flavoured with chaat masala (spices) and sugar.

Gulab Jamun

Gulab jamun is a very famous Pakistani dessert. Its soft, fried dumplings traditionally made of thickened or reduced milk, soaked in rose flavored sugar syrup. It will just melt in your mouth! Gulab Jamun looks like Timbits soaked in sweet syrup.

Jalebi

Jalebi is made by deep frying flour batter in circular shape which is then soaked in, a sweet Syrup. The sweets are served warm or cold. Some people also eat jalebi with milk.

Kheer

Kheer is like rice pudding which made by boiling rice with milk and sugar. it is flavoured with cardamom, raisins, pistachios or almonds. It is typically served during a meal or as a dessert.

Pakora

A good Ramadan snack (especially for vegetarians) pakora combines vegetables with flour. They're also fried so they have a crunchy texture.

Samosa

Little pastries that can be filled with either meat or vegetables, samosas can be frozen and defrosted and fried later. You can either buy the pastry or you can make your own from scratch.
