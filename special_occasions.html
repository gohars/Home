Special Occasion Dishes

The foods on this page are considered foods for special occasion and are not foods you want to be eating on a daily basis. The foods here are dishes I would recommend to anyone who wants a taste of Pakistani Cuisine.

Haleem

It may look like brown soup but haleem is actually a mixture of meat and lentils typically topped with lemon, fried onions, chillis, coriander, and ginger pieces.

Nihari

Although it's traditionally made with lamb, nihari can also be made with chicken or a meat substitute. It's typically served with naan bread and sprinkled with coriander, cut chillis, onions, ginger pieces, and lemon juice.

Paya

It is a very famous Pakistani dish. Paya means feet. The main ingredients of the dish are the trotters of either goat, cow or sheep. It is slow cooked with water and lots of spices. It's usually eaten with roti or naan.
